Privacy Policy

Daily Devotional Notes — Your Sacred Personal Journal

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

Your privacy matters deeply to us. This app was built as a sacred personal journal — your devotional notes, prayers, and reflections are yours alone. This policy explains what we collect and how we protect your privacy.

1. Who We Are

Daily Devotional Notes is a personal Bible journaling app created by R. Dalisay. The app is available at nikkanikka031-hash.github.io/daily-devotional-notes and is intended for personal, non-commercial use by individuals who wish to build a daily habit of Bible reading, prayer, and reflection.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect minimal data. Here is a full breakdown:

  • Your Name — Entered voluntarily during first-time setup. Stored locally on your device only. Never sent to any server.
  • Devotional Journal Entries — Your Bible readings, notes, prayers, and reflections. Stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. Never transmitted or accessible to anyone else.
  • Habit Tracker & Streak Data — Your daily habit completions and streak count. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Testimonies & Feedback — If you choose to submit a testimony, the text and date are stored in a secure database (Supabase). No personal identifying information (name, email, device ID) is attached to testimonies.
  • Anonymous Usage Analytics — We use Google Analytics to count how many people visit the app. This collects anonymous data such as page views, device type, and general location (country/city). No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • User Token — A randomly generated anonymous ID stored on your device used only to allow you to delete your own testimonies. It contains no personal information.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your email address
  • We do NOT collect your phone number
  • We do NOT collect passwords
  • We do NOT sell your data to anyone
  • We do NOT share your personal data with third parties
  • We do NOT read your personal journal entries
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising

4. How Your Data is Stored

Local Data (Journal, Habits, Name): Stored in your browser's localStorage on your own device. This data never leaves your device. It is only accessible by you through the app on that specific device and browser.

Testimonies: Stored in a secure Supabase database hosted in Northeast Asia (Seoul, South Korea). Only the testimony text and submission date are stored — no name, no email, no device information.

Analytics: Processed by Google Analytics. Data is anonymized and aggregated. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data — Retained indefinitely on your device until you clear your browser data or uninstall the app.
  • Testimonies — Retained indefinitely unless you delete them yourself using the delete button in the app, or request deletion by contacting us.
  • Analytics — Retained according to Google Analytics default retention settings (14 months).

6. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages and contains no harmful content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your own testimonies at any time using the delete button in the app
  • Clear all your local data by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Request deletion of any testimony by contacting us
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking

8. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Database for shared testimonies. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous usage tracking. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Netlify — App hosting. Privacy policy: netlify.com/privacy
  • Google Fonts — Typography. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
